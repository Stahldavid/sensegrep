# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in the sensegrep community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and discuss ideas on their merits.
- Give and accept actionable technical feedback.
- Focus on improving the project and helping users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or discrimination.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

To report conduct issues, open a private maintainer contact issue:
- https://github.com/Stahldavid/sensegrep/issues/new
